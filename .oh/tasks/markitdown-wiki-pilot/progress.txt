- Wiki corpus entity writes remain orchestrator-only; sub-agents may implement references and probes but must leave curated corpus/index promotion to the parent.
- A pinned `uvx` acquisition/version preflight can populate cache without source access; source conversion can then set `UV_OFFLINE=1` under hard process limits.
