## Codebase Patterns

- `/drift-check` is a read-only skill: recommended reschedule/restart commands may be printed, but the skill must never execute them.
- Cron body edits and cron frontmatter/config edits have different lifecycles: `reloadBody()` hot-reloads body text at fire time, while scheduler/config fields require SIGHUP reschedule or runtime restart.
