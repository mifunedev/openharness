## Codebase Patterns

- Worktree-local test runs can symlink the root `node_modules` for vitest, but the symlink is local-only and must not be committed.
- Shared root runtime logs should be appended through `scripts/locked-append.sh`; helper failures in preflight logging must not change cap/status decisions.
