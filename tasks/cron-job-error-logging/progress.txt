- **Line numbers in the PRD drift.** The PRD/prd.json reference "line 180" for the `new Cron(...)` call, but the live `scripts/cron-runtime.ts` has it at ~line 198/206 (the file grew). Locate call sites by content (`grep -n "new Cron"`, `grep -n "catch: true"`), not by the PRD's stated line number.

Prefer logger injection for determinism.
