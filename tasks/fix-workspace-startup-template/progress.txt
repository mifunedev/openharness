- `workspace/` is an intentionally minimal agent-runtime template; app/docs dev servers should be launched explicitly by users or harness packs, not by a hidden root entrypoint hook.

- The boot-lint CI job and `evals/probes/boot-lint-glob.sh` must track the active shell-script set. Removing the only `workspace/*.sh` file means the old glob must be removed too.
