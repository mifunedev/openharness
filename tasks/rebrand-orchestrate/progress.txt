## Codebase Patterns

- Protected skill edits are allowed only when the PRD carries an explicit override; keep them to the named non-destructive command-reference replacements and avoid restructuring the protected skill.
- After a `git mv` directory rename, re-stage the destination file after content edits before committing; otherwise the commit can capture only the pure rename and leave identity edits unstaged.
- Eval contract probes deliberately pin non-command contract literals; during skill rebrands, update paths/messages/frontmatter assertions while leaving the literal array unchanged unless the story says otherwise.
